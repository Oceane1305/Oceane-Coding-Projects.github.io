How to prepare African Jollof Rice

Jollof rice, is a rice dish from West Africa.The dish is typically made with long-grain rice, tomatoes, onions, spices, vegetables and meat in a single pot, although its ingredients and preparation methods vary across different regions

Here are the steps of how to cook Jollof Rice in your kitchen

Jollof rice is made of vegetable oil, onion, ginger, pressed garlic cloves, chillies, tomato paste, beef or goat meat or chicken (some times alternated with mixed vegetables), local or refined rice, typically jasmine rice and black pepper

The method of cooking jollof begins with first preparing the beef or chicken by seasoning and steaming it with a pureé of ginger, onions and garlic and frying it until it is well-cooked. The rest of the ingredients are then fried all together, starting from onions, pepper, tomato paste, tomatoes and spices in that order. After all the ingredients have been fried, rice is then added and cooked until the meal is prepared. Ghanaian jollof is typically served with side dishes of beef, chicken, well-seasoned fried fish, or mixed vegetables

To begin: Get your ingredients/ 8 people

  • 1 large/big chicken
  • 5 tomatoes
  • 2 onions
  • 5 tablespoons peanut oil
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato puree
  • 2 small gold Kub (Maggi)
  • 3 small peppers (optional) or 1/2 teaspoon powdered Espelette pepper
  • 5 rice mustard glasses
  • Plantain

Preparation

The method of cooking jollof begins with first preparing the beef or chicken by seasoning and steaming it with a pureé of ginger, onions and garlic and frying it until it is well-cooked. The rest of the ingredients are then fried all together, starting from onions, pepper, tomato paste, tomatoes and spices in that order. After all the ingredients have been fried, rice is then added and cooked until the meal is prepared. Ghanaian jollof is typically served with side dishes of beef, chicken, well-seasoned fried fish, or mixed vegetables

Cooking steps

  • Step 1

    • Sauté the chopped onions in the oil. Add the pieces of meat, the seeded and chopped tomatoes and the tomato puree.

    When everything is well seized, pour 2 liters of water. Bring to a boil, put the Kub gold. Cook over medium heat for 30 minutes. Add the peppers 10 minutes before the end of cooking (not the Espelette pepper).

  • Step 2

    • Cook your chicken in the oven in 1 dish with oil or margarine, pepper and salt. (cooking at 180°C, thermostat 6, about 1 hour for a whole chicken).

    When it is well cooked, remove the flesh of the chicken in small pieces and set aside.

  • Step 3

    • In a saucepan over the heat, pour the fatty cooking sauce for the chicken.

    Bring the preparation to a rolling boil, throw in the rice, washed beforehand. Stir with a wooden spoon and let boil for 5 minutes uncovered, then lower the heat, cover without touching the rice. After 20 minutes the rice is cooked with loose grains.

  • Step 4

    • Let it cook like 1 rice and depending on its cooking time, it all depends on the rice you are using, but about 15 minutes for long grain rice.>You don't have to put in all your chicken.

    How to serve Jollof Rice

    Serve the rice, meat and broth separately. You can also eat Jollof rice with plantain accompanied by another meat other than chicken.
